Biryani Recipe

Biryani is one of our all-time favorite dishes! Here's how you can make this delicious and flavorful dish at home.

Ingredients

For the Rice:

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 4 cups water
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 4-5 cloves
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • Salt to taste

For the Masala:

  1. 500g chicken (or your choice of meat/vegetables)
  2. 2 large onions (sliced)
  3. 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  4. 1 cup yogurt
  5. 3 tbsp biryani masala powder
  6. 1 tsp turmeric powder
  7. 1 tsp red chili powder
  8. 3 tbsp cooking oil or ghee
  9. Fresh coriander and mint leaves (chopped)

How to Make Biryani

Step 1: Wash and soak the basmati rice for 30 minutes. Cook the rice with bay leaves, cloves, cinnamon, and salt until 70% done. Drain and set aside.

Step 2: Heat oil or ghee in a pan. Saut√© onions until golden brown. Add tomatoes and cook until soft.

Step 3: Add chicken, biryani masala, turmeric, red chili powder, and yogurt. Cook until the chicken is tender and the masala thickens.

Step 4: In a large pot, layer the cooked rice and chicken masala. Sprinkle fresh coriander and mint leaves between layers.

Step 5: Cover and cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes (dum cooking) to let the flavors combine.

Step 6: Serve hot with raita or your favorite side dish!

Enjoy your homemade biryani!
